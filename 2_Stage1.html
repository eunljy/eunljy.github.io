What is Generative AI?
Definition: Generative AI (GenAI) refers to artificial intelligence models that create new content like text, images, music, and videos, by learning from existing data.
Enhanced content creation GenAI serves as a powerful tool for artists, designers, musicians, and writers by providing inspiration and generating new ideas.
Boosts innovation across industries In areas like healthcare, AI-powered diagnostic tools detect diseases earlier and personalize treatment plans.
Increased efficiency AI-powered tools assist developers by autocompleting code, identifying errors, and optimizing performance. AI identifies trends in seconds.
