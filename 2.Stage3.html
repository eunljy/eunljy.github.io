How do we use GenAI responsibly?
With great power comes great responsibility! To ensure GenAI remains a valuable asset instead of a hazard, we must adopt good user accountability. Click the boxes below to find out more!
Verify Information and Cross-check

✔ Actively verify AI-generated outputs by cross-referencing with reputable sources

✔ Fact-check with other perspectives

✔ Consult subject matter experts when necessary
Maintain Transparency
Organizations and individuals using AI should clearly label and cite AI-generated text, images, or videos to inform audiences of their origin.
Ensure Ethical Use

✔ Adhere to policies that prohibit the creation of harmful or deceptive content

✔ Obtain consent before generating AI-based works involving real individuals

✔ Respect intellectual property rights
